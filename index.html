Welcome to My Personal Webpage

I am a third-year PhD student in Applied Linguistics at the University of Birmingham, working under the supervision of Jack Grieve and Akira Murakami. My research, titled Style in the Eye of Machine: Exploring Authorial Styles with Large Language Models, investigates how computational methods, including large language models, can analyze and interpret authorial styles. My research interests include linguistics, computational techniques, and stylistics.
